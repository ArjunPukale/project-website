Manjsuhree

Bronze statue, cast using the Lost Wax method, with gold painting. The painted details on this Manjsuhree's face are beautiful. These statues have been selected by Prajwol in Nepal. He has been coordinating with our fourth generation statue-makers in Lalitpur. Bottom is removable for consecration. Size: 8 inches tall

Chenrezig Statue

This exquisite Chenrezig statue is handmade in Kathmandu Nepal by the Shakya family. This family has been making bronze statues for many generations and is well known all over the world. Each bronze statue is made using the lost wax method, which takes considerable time and skill. After casting each statue is hand detailed and carved by master craftsmen and women. The statue is painted with gold and inlaid with turquoise and gemstones. Size: 13 inches tall
