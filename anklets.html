Riverstone Wrist

There is an elegance in the simplicity of this matte riverstone wrist mala. Great earthy, woodsy colors to accentuate a natural style. Riverstone's energy is said to accelerate change. It is very useful when performing meditation or therapy- it helps to move more swiftly through the process and focuses on energy to do so. Adjustable wrist mala measures approximately 6 inches when closed and 8 1/2 when opened.

Thailand Mala

A simple, yet meaningful mala. We sourced this beautiful oiled rosewood from Thailand and hand strung them on stretchy cord. A simple statement for men or women, it looks great worn on its own or in combination with other malas and bracelets. Associated with the heart chakra, rosewood is known for its compassionate and healing energies. Bracelet comes in size small and large on stretchy cord. Beads are 8mm. Small measures approximately 7 inches around, large is about 8 inches around.
